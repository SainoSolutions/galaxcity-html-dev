Our Story

Operating since 18 July 2016 and proudly based in Imphal, we are more than just an electronics store—we’re a place where you can truly experience technology. At our store, it’s not just about buying a device—it’s about experiencing it first. We’ve created a space where customers can see, feel, and try products before making a decision. We deal with premium brands known for their quality, reliability, and strong after-sales support, offering everything from smartphones and accessories to smart devices and audio gear. We’ve also extended this experience-first approach to our social media platforms, where we create content that gives you a real, POV-style feel of using a product—helping you understand its potential before you even purchase it. We don’t just focus on sales—we focus on helping our customers make confident choices and enjoy a seamless tech journey from start to finish.
